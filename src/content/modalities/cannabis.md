Cannabis is the most everyday item on this page, and also one of the least
predictable. The biphasic pattern — calming low, activating high — means it
doesn't map cleanly onto "relaxation," even when that's what people reach for
it for.

Held honestly: for some people, in small amounts, it opens a door to feeling
more in the body. For others, or at higher doses, it does the opposite and
winds the mind up. It's worth being clear-eyed about which one you tend to get,
and about the real risks (dependence, and psychosis vulnerability in some
people) rather than assuming it settles the nervous system by default.
