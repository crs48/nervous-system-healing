Coherent breathing just means slowing your breath to a smooth, even rhythm of
roughly five to six breaths a minute — around five or six seconds in, five or
six seconds out. At that pace, heart rate and breath tend to fall into sync,
and heart rate variability (a rough marker of a flexible, resilient nervous
system) goes up.

It's the most accessible thing in this whole directory: free, needs no
equipment or teacher, and you can do it anywhere. Honesty matters here,
though — the quick physiological effect is real, but the bigger claims that
circulate on social media outrun the evidence. Treat it as a genuinely useful
tool and a good on-ramp, not a treatment. You can try it right now on the
[breathing pacer](/nervous-system-healing/tools/breathing/).
