TRE is a short sequence of exercises designed to gently fatigue certain
muscles until the body begins a natural, self-limiting tremor — the same kind
of shaking animals do to discharge stress, and that people sometimes do after
a scare. The idea is that letting that tremor happen releases deep, held
tension.

It's always meant to be self-regulated: you can stop the tremors any time
simply by straightening your legs or standing up, and short is strongly
encouraged over long. Because thawing can bring up a lot, this is one where
the "start small, and with guidance" advice really matters — see the
contraindications above.
