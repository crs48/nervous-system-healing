**This takes about 3 minutes. You'll name things you can sense, out loud or
in your head. No sound needed, eyes open.**

Working down from five to one, and going as slowly as you like:

- **Five** things you can **see**. Ordinary is perfect — a mug, a scuff on
  the wall, the light on the floor.
- **Four** things you can **feel** — your feet in your socks, the chair
  against your back, air on your hands.
- **Three** things you can **hear** — near or far, even the quiet ones.
- **Two** things you can **smell** (or two you like the smell of, if the air
  is neutral).
- **One** thing you can **taste**, or one slow breath if there's nothing.

That's it. If your mind wanders off partway, that's not failing — just pick
up wherever you left off, or start again. You can stop any time.
