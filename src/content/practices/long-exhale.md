**This takes about 2 minutes. You'll let your out-breaths get a little
longer. No sound; do it however you're sitting or lying.**

1. Don't change anything at first — just notice you're breathing. It's been
   happening this whole time without you.
2. When you're ready, on your next out-breath, let it be a touch longer and
   slower than the breath in. You don't need to force air out or empty your
   lungs — just let the exhale unspool.
3. Let the in-breath come whenever it wants, no effort. Then a slightly long
   exhale again. A rough four-in, six-out is a common shape, but don't count
   if counting adds pressure — the feeling of a slightly longer exhale is the
   whole point.
4. Six or eight breaths is plenty.

If breath-focus feels uncomfortable — for some people it does — stop, and try
[looking slowly around the room](../orienting/) instead. Nothing here is
required.
