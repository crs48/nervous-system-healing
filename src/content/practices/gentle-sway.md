**This takes about 3 minutes. You'll rock or sway slowly. No sound, in any
position — sitting, standing, or lying down.**

1. However you're positioned, start the smallest sway — side to side, or
   gently front to back. Barely anything at first.
2. Let it find a rhythm that feels good, like you're being rocked. Slow is
   nicer than big. Your eyes can be open or closed, whatever's comfortable.
3. If a hand wants to rest on your chest or belly as you sway, let it. If a
   hum or a sigh joins in, let that too.
4. Let the movement be its own thing for a minute or two. There's nowhere to
   get to.
5. When you're ready, let the sway slowly get smaller until it stops on its
   own, and notice how you feel.

Rocking, fidgeting, or stimming however your body likes is exactly right
here. Stop whenever you want.
